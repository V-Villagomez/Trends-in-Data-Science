Data Scientist Career Trends in the United States




The U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics projects a positive employment percent change of 31.4 between 2020 through 2030 for Data Scientists. According to the Economic News Release published on September 8, 2021, the demand for new products associated with the Internet of Things (IoT), and for analyzing and interpreting large datasets are expected to contribute to fast employment growth for occupations which include statisticians, information security analysts, and data scientists.
